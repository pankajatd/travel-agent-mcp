Here's a suggested 7-day itinerary for your Japan trip:

Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo

* Arrive at Narita or Haneda airport
* Check-in to your hotel and freshen up
* Visit the famous Shibuya Crossing and take a walk around the Shibuya area
* Enjoy a traditional Japanese dinner at a local restaurant

Day 2: Tokyo

* Visit the Tokyo Tower for panoramic views of the city
* Explore the Asakusa district, including Senso-ji Temple and Nakamise Shopping Street
* Take a stroll in the Imperial Palace East Garden
* Enjoy a sumo wrestling tournament at the Ryogoku Kokugikan (optional)

Day 3: Tokyo

* Visit the Meiji Shrine, a serene Shinto shrine located in a peaceful forested area
* Take a walk in the trendy Harajuku district and see the fashionable youth culture
* Visit the Ghibli Museum, dedicated to the works of Studio Ghibli and its founder Hayao Miyazaki (optional)
* Enjoy a traditional Japanese tea ceremony

Day 4: Kyoto

* Take a bullet train (Shinkansen) to Kyoto
* Visit the Fushimi Inari Shrine, famous for its thousands of vermillion torii gates
* Explore the Kiyomizu-dera Temple, a UNESCO World Heritage site and one of the most famous temples in Japan
* Enjoy a traditional Kyoto-style dinner

Day 5: Kyoto

* Visit the Arashiyama Bamboo Grove and take a stroll along the Katsura River
* Explore the Nijo Castle, a UNESCO World Heritage site and former residence of the Tokugawa shoguns
* Visit the Kinkaku-ji Temple (Golden Pavilion), a stunning temple with a beautiful garden
* Enjoy a traditional Japanese garden tour

Day 6: Osaka

* Take a train to Osaka
* Visit the Osaka Castle, which offers great views of the city
* Explore the Dotonbori area, known for its colorful neon lights and street food
* Enjoy a traditional Osaka-style dinner

Day 7: Departure from Osaka

* Spend the morning shopping for last-minute souvenirs or visiting any attractions you missed earlier
* Depart from Kansai International Airport or Osaka's Itami Airport
