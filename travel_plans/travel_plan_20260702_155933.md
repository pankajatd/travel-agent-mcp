Here's a suggested 7-day itinerary for your Japan trip:

Day 1: Arrival in Tokyo

* Arrive at Narita or Haneda airport
* Check-in to your hotel and freshen up
* Visit the famous Shibuya Crossing and take a walk around the Shibuya area
* Enjoy a traditional Japanese dinner at a local restaurant

Day 2: Tokyo

* Visit the Tokyo Tower for panoramic views of the city
* Explore the Asakusa district, including Senso-ji Temple and Nakamise Shopping Street
* Visit the Imperial Palace, the main residence of the Emperor of Japan
* Enjoy a traditional Japanese tea ceremony

Day 3: Tokyo

* Visit the Tsukiji Outer Market for a sushi-making experience and fresh seafood
* Take a stroll in the beautiful Hamarikyu Gardens
* Visit the Meiji Shrine, a serene Shinto shrine located in a tranquil forested area
* Explore the trendy Harajuku district and see the fashionable youth culture

Day 4: Tokyo to Nikko

* Take a day trip to Nikko, a UNESCO World Heritage site located about 2 hours away from Tokyo
* Visit the Toshogu Shrine, a magnificent Shinto shrine dedicated to Tokugawa Ieyasu
* Explore the Rinno-ji Temple and the Futarasan Shrine
* Enjoy a traditional Japanese lunch in Nikko

Day 5: Tokyo

* Visit the Ghibli Museum, dedicated to the works of Studio Ghibli and its founder Hayao Miyazaki
* Explore the trendy Daikanyama district and visit the trendy shops and cafes
* Visit the teamLab Borderless digital art museum in Odaiba
* Enjoy a traditional Japanese dinner and watch a sumo wrestling match (optional)

Day 6: Tokyo

* Visit the Tokyo National Museum, which houses a vast collection of Japanese art and artifacts
* Explore the beautiful Shinjuku Gyoen National Garden
* Visit the trendy Shimokitazawa district and explore the small shops and cafes
* Enjoy a farewell dinner at a Michelin-starred restaurant (optional)

Day 7: Departure from Tokyo

* Spend the morning shopping for last-minute souvenirs or visiting any last-minute sights
* Depart for the airport and fly back home
